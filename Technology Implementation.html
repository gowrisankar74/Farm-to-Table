Software Components

Deployment Strategy

The traceability solution will be deployed in three phases:

  1. Pilot phase: A small-scale test of the system with a single product line and a limited number of participants.
  2. Rollout phase: A gradual expansion of the system to additional product lines and participants, with a focus on training and support.
  3. Scaling phase: A full-scale deployment of the system across all product lines and participants, with ongoing monitoring and optimization.

Testing and Validation Process

  • Unit testing: Testing of individual hardware and software components to ensure they meet the specifications and requirements.
  • Integration testing: Testing of the integrated system to ensure it functions as a whole and meets the business needs.
  • User acceptance testing: Testing by end-users to ensure the system meets their expectations and usability requirements.
  • Performance testing: Testing to evaluate the system's speed, reliability, and scalability under various conditions.
