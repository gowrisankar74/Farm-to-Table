Quality Assurance Plan - Traceability Solution

Quality Control Checks

  • All RFID readers, barcode scanners, GPS devices, and sensors will be tested and calibrated regularly to ensure accuracy and reliability.
  • All data capture points will be monitored and audited to detect and prevent errors, omissions, or discrepancies.
  • All data will be checked for completeness, consistency, and validity before being stored in the data management system.

Data Validation Methods

  • Data validation will be performed using automated and manual methods to detect and correct errors and inconsistencies.
  • Data will be compared against established standards, such as product specifications, regulatory requirements, and industry best practices.
  • Data validation will include cross-checks between different data sources and data points to ensure data integrity and traceability.

Corrective Actions

  • If data errors or inconsistencies are detected, corrective actions will be taken immediately to prevent further impact on the supply chain.
  • Data errors and corrective actions will be documented and tracked to ensure full traceability and accountability.
  • The root cause of data errors will be identified and addressed to prevent recurrence.
